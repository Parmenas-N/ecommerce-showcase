Orders & Shipping

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our Shop page, choose the product you want, click "Add to Cart", and proceed to checkout. You'll be guided through the process step by step.

We offer free shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, shipping costs $5. Delivery typically takes 3-5 business days within Kenya.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the courier's website.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. Items must be in original condition.

To return an item, please contact us with your order number and the reason for return. We will send you return instructions and a shipping label if applicable.

Once we receive your returned item, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.
